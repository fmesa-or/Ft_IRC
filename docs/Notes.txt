Use SIGINIT and SIGQUIT to handle 'Ctrl + C' and 'ctrl + \'

Ports:
	[0 to 1023] ->		Reserved for specific services and protocols:
							- 80 -> HTTP
							- 21 -> FTP
							- 22 -> SSH
						They require administrative privileges to use.

	[1024 to 49151] ->	Can be registered for specific purposes and are used by non-standard applications and services.

	[49152 to 65535]->	Dynamic or private ports. Used by client applications for outgoing connections.
						These ports are dynamically allocated by the operating system to clients when they initiate outgoing connections.
